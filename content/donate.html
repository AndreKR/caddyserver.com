FAQ

Are my donations private?

Yes. Your donations are completely private and are not recorded or tracked by the Caddy project in any way. It's simply person-to-person.

Can I donate to the project itself?

By donating to developers, you do fund the project. Donations allow developers to devote more time to the project. To keep things simple and give you more influence, there is no collective pool of money for the whole project.

Are my donations tax-deductable?

Probably not. We're individuals, not non-profit organizations. But you'll still feel good inside.

What do you do with my donations?

That depends on the recipient. Some have day jobs, others don't. Those who don't will use your donation to cover living expenses so they can continue to build Caddy. Those who do will find more motivation to work on the project, thus improving the quality of the software.
